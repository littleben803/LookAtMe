LookAtMe App

Privacy Policy

LookAtMe is a local-first LED banner tool. We do not provide accounts, servers, ad tracking, or online AI features.

Effective Date: June 15, 2026 Applies To: Version 2.0.0 and later

Data We Process

The banner text you enter, your saved favorites, and your display settings are stored locally on your device so the app can restore your frequently used banners and display preferences.

This data is not automatically uploaded to our servers when you use LookAtMe. The current version does not provide account login, cloud sync, community publishing, or online AI features.

Local Storage And Deletion

  • Saved favorites help you quickly reopen commonly used banners on the same device.
  • Preferences such as colors, fonts, scroll speed, landscape display, and keeping the screen awake are used to restore your display experience.
  • Clearing the cache does not delete saved favorites by default. Favorites are deleted only after you explicitly confirm that action.

Pro Benefits And Payment Information

LookAtMe Pro is an in-app purchase. Purchase, restore, refund, and payment processing are provided by Apple App Store.

We do not access or store your payment account, bank card information, Apple ID password, or complete transaction credentials. The app uses StoreKit to verify purchase eligibility under the current Apple ID, and may store a local entitlement cache for faster interface updates. Final entitlement status is determined by Apple's verification result.

Third-Party Services

The current version does not integrate advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, third-party login SDKs, or self-operated servers. In-app purchases are provided by Apple StoreKit and App Store.

Children's Privacy

LookAtMe provides a local banner display tool for general users and does not knowingly collect children's personal information. If you believe related content needs attention, please contact us through the support page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how data is handled, please visit our support page for contact information.
